Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any coding experience?

Not at all! Mumble Global is designed for absolute beginners. We start with visual blocks and gradually introduce text-based coding. Many of our learners had never touched code before and are now building their own projects!

Is the platform really senior-friendly?

Absolutely! We've designed every aspect with seniors in mind: large text options, high contrast mode, voice guidance, simple navigation, and a dedicated support team trained to assist seniors patiently.

How does crowd-sourced data help me learn?

Our community shares real coding examples, tips, and project ideas. You learn from peers who've faced similar challenges. All content is moderated for quality and safety, so you get the best wisdom from thousands of senior coders.

What if I need help or get stuck?

We're here for you! Call our friendly support line at 1-800-MUMBLE-GO, email us, or use the contact form. We also have live video office hours where you can speak directly with coaches.
