Frequently Asked Questions

How long is each coaching session?

Each session is 60 minutes. You can schedule up to 4 sessions per month (weekly).

Can I choose my coach?

Yes! You can select your preferred coach based on their availability and specialty. You can also switch coaches anytime if you'd like to work with someone else.

What if I miss a session?

We understand things come up. You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your session with no penalty. If you miss without notice, you'll still have your remaining sessions for the month.

Can I cancel my coaching subscription?

Absolutely! You can cancel anytime with 7 days notice. No long-term contracts. We're confident you'll love the experience!

Is coaching suitable for absolute beginners?

Yes! Our coaches specialize in working with beginners. They're patient, encouraging, and skilled at explaining concepts in simple, easy-to-understand ways.
